Frequently Asked Questions


1. What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive AI-driven analytics and alarm systems focused on identifying fraud patterns in litigation within the insurance sector. Our services include data analysis, model development, and real-time alarm triggering for potential fraud cases.

2. How do you source your data?

We source data from open court orders and pending petitions across India. This data is carefully curated to ensure accuracy and relevance for our analytics and fraud detection systems.

3. What is the purpose of your alarm system?

Our alarm system is designed to identify and alert users of new cases that match patterns associated with fraud. This helps in proactive risk management and enhances decision-making in the insurance sector.
